About Dr. Omar

Bridging Western Medical Excellence with Regional Understanding

I am Dr. Omar Dhaimat, a Consultant Endocrinologist dedicated to untangling the complexities of the human endocrine system. With over 23 years of clinical experience spanning the United States, Jordan, and the UAE, I bring a global perspective to metabolic health.

My journey is defined by a pursuit of rigor. After earning my medical degree from the University of Jordan, I completed my Residency in Internal Medicine at Seton Hall University and my Fellowship in Endocrinology & Metabolism at the prestigious University of Miami (USA). This foundation led to my Double American Board Certification—a standard of excellence I maintain to ensure my patients receive world-class care.

Currently serving at Clemenceau Medical Centre (CMC) Dubai, I focus on "Precision Endocrinology"—utilizing the latest in diabetes technology (sensors and pumps) and advanced thyroid management to restore balance to my patients' lives. I believe that true healing occurs when high-tech medical solutions meet high-touch, compassionate listening.

“I believe in the 'EQ and IQ' of medicine. It is not enough to possess the intellectual knowledge (IQ) to diagnose a condition; a physician must also have the emotional intelligence (EQ) to understand how that condition affects the patient's daily life. My practice is built on listening first, then treating.”

— Dr. Omar Dhaimat
